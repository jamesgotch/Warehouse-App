About WMS

A full-stack inventory management app for a sports warehouse. Built with FastAPI, SQLModel, and SQLite on the backend, and a vanilla HTML/CSS/JS frontend served as static files.

Dashboard Stats

StatDescription
Total SKUsNumber of unique inventory records
Total UnitsSum of all stock quantities
Total ValueCombined value of all stock (quantity × price)
CategoriesNumber of distinct product categories
Low StockItems with fewer than 20 units remaining

API Endpoints

MethodEndpointDescription
GET/inventoryReturn all records
GET/inventory/{id}Return a single record
POST/inventoryCreate a new record
PUT/inventory/{id}Update a record
DELETE/inventory/{id}Delete a record
GET/statsReturn dashboard statistics

Database Schema

ColumnTypeNotes

brandTEXTe.g. Nike, Adidas, Wilson

colorTEXTe.g. Black, White, Grey
quantityINTEGERUnits in stock, defaults to 0
priceREALUnit price in USD
